Super Resolution

TensorFlow

Definition

Super Resolution (SR) is a compilation of techniques for optimizing and maximizing image and video frame resolution. Through various algorithms and methods for modifying the properties of frames, this technique has provided important contributions to computer vision. This results in further data retrieval from frames.

TensorFlow Lite

Introduction

Image Super-resolution attempts to transform a low-resolution image with coarse details into a comparable high resolution image with improved visual accuracy and refined details. Image super resolution is defined using terminology such as image scaling, zooming, up sampling, interpolation, and enlargement. A single image or a series of images may be used to create a higher-resolution raster image.
